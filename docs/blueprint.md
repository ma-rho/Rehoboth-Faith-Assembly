# **App Name**: Rehoboth Connect

## Core Features:

- Welcome and Key Information Display: A responsive home page featuring a hero section, brief church introduction, service times preview, and calls-to-action for visiting, donating, and contacting.
- Dynamic Events Listing: Fetch and display upcoming church events with details such as title, date, and description from a Firestore database.
- Sermon Video Archive: Present a dynamic list of past sermons, each embedded with a YouTube video and including the sermon title and date, retrieved from a Firestore database.
- About Us & Leadership: Dedicated pages outlining the church's mission, vision, and providing an introduction to the pastor.
- Online Giving via Stripe: A simple donation page integrating Stripe Payment Links for secure online contributions, without requiring a custom backend.
- Contact Form Submissions: A contact form for visitors to send messages, with submissions stored in a Firestore database and user-friendly success/error states.
- Comprehensive Service Schedule: A page dedicated to displaying the weekly service schedule and detailed church location information, with a placeholder for an embedded map.

## Style Guidelines:

- Primary Color: A deep, reassuring blue (#24448F) for conveying trust and community, complementing the requested navy scheme.
- Background Color: A soft, almost white blue-grey (#EEF0F3) providing a clean and accessible canvas for content, maintaining visual harmony with the primary color.
- Accent Color: A vibrant sky blue (#4DCCE3) to highlight calls-to-action and important information, ensuring strong contrast and user guidance.
- Font: 'PT Sans' (sans-serif) for all text. Its modern yet warm characteristics ensure high readability and a welcoming feel across headlines and body content, suitable for all audiences.
- Utilize simple, clean, and line-based icons throughout the site to enhance navigation and comprehension without clutter, maintaining a modern and accessible aesthetic.
- Implement a mobile-first, responsive design with a clean, intuitive layout that prioritizes ease of use. Employ generous whitespace for clarity and readability, ensuring optimal viewing across all devices.
- Incorporate subtle, tasteful animations for transitions and interactive elements, enhancing the user experience without being distracting or compromising performance.
